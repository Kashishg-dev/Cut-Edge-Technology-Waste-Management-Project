Waste Plastic Recycle to Making Shoes

Transforming West's plastic waste into eco-friendly shoes. We're committed to sustainability, using recycled plastic to craft durable, stylish footwear. Join us in reducing waste and treading lightly on the environment.

How It Works

Step 1

We collect e-waste and batteries from individuals, businesses, and partners through safe and regulated channels.

Step 2

Devices are carefully dismantled by experts to separate recyclable materials from hazardous components.

Step 3

Using eco-conscious technology, we extract metals, plastics, and rare elements for reuse.

Step 4

Recovered materials are refined and repurposed into viable industrial-grade components.
